Monstera

The Monstera, or "Swiss Cheese Plant," is a tropical houseplant known for its large, holey leaves. Native to Central and South America, it thrives in indirect light and adds an exotic touch to any space.

Snake plant

The Snake Plant, or "Mother-in-Law's Tongue," is a hardy, low-maintenance houseplant with upright, sword-like leaves. It thrives in various light conditions and improves indoor air quality.

Cactus

Small cactuses are low-maintenance, sun-loving plants with unique shapes and spiky textures. Perfect for adding charm to any space with minimal care.
